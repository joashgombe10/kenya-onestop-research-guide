## So you have a research idea you want to pursue in Kenya.

Here is the clearest, most up‑to‑date pathway, based on official requirements as of January 2025.

### Step 1: Identify your Institutional Review Board (IRB)

If you are affiliated with a Kenyan university or research institution, start with their accredited IRB.

### Step 2: County clearance

Many researchers forget this step. You need a letter from the County Director of Health.

### Step 3: NACOSTI research permit

Submit your IRB approval and county clearance via the NACOSTI online portal.
